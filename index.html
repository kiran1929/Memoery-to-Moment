What We Actually Do

Three pillars.
One unforgettable moment.

We handle the plan, the people, and the proof — so you just walk in and take all the credit.

The Surprise Planner

The conceptualization and styling phase — we build the idea and run the full setup so the surprise lands perfectly.

💍 Romantic Milestones
  • "Flash" Proposals — Orchestrating a sudden flash mob, choir, or candlelit setup at a restaurant to pop the question in style.
  • Private Dining Transformations — Turning an ordinary rooftop or backyard into a luxury, candle-lit dinner experience.
🎂 Family & Friends
  • "Midnight Ambush" Birthdays — Decorating a room, office, or cubicle before the person arrives, turning the ordinary into the extraordinary.
  • Nostalgia Drops — Tracking down childhood friends and coordinating their surprise appearance at an event for maximum emotional impact.
🏢 Corporate / Workplace
  • Promotion Celebrations — Surprising an employee with a tailored desk makeover and a premium gift hamper to honour the milestone.
  • Welcome-back & Farewell Gestures — Marking big career transitions in a way the team will never forget.
🕊️ Apology & Reconciliation

Curated "I'm Sorry" experiences — featuring live acoustic musicians, personalized art installations, or meaningful gestures delivered directly and discreetly to the person.

The Memory Maker

Our photo and video arm — split into two modes based on the scale of your event.

A · Instant Services For short, high-impact events
📸 The "Paparazzi" Capture

A hidden photographer booked for 30–60 minutes captures the exact raw moment — the gasp, the tears — from the bushes or across the street.

🎬 On-the-Spot Reel Creators

A videographer shoots on mobile, edits with trending music, and hands over a 30-second social-media-ready vertical reel within one hour.

📷 Polaroid / Instant Print Drops

A photographer snaps the surprise moment and instantly hands the couple or family a stack of physical Polaroid photos before leaving.

B · Long Event Services For multi-day events
🎥 Documentary-Style Follows

A crew shadows a family or group for an entire weekend trip or 2-day celebration, capturing the behind-the-scenes buildup and the main event.

🚁 Cinematic After-Movies

High-end production with drone footage, multi-camera setups, and professional lighting — crafted for large-scale milestone events.

📖 Coffee Table Book Curation

We don't just shoot — we physically print, bind, and deliver a premium hard-cover photo album weeks later as a permanent keepsake.

Event Fulfillment

The "Done-For-You" execution — we combine the Planner, the Memory Maker, and real on-ground heavy lifting so the client doesn't have to lift a single finger.

📍 Venue & Logistics Scouting

We secure permits for public proposals, book private dining rooms, and organize luxury transport — like a vintage car or premium chauffeur — to get the person to the location without them suspecting a thing.

🌸 Local Artisan Sourcing

We book the independent local baker for the custom cake, the freelance florist for rare flowers, and the indie musician — ensuring the client gets unique, non-commercial goods sourced directly from Bengaluru's best.

🕵️ The "Shadow" Coordinator

A real person acts as the invisible stage manager on the day — hiding at the venue, sending "Are you 5 minutes away?" texts, cueing the musician to start, and signalling the hidden photographer. The client feels zero stress.

🥂 Premium White-Glove Orchestration

Our highest-ticket tier for high-net-worth clients — end-to-end luxury including private helicopter bookings, yacht rentals, or exclusive venue takeovers, all orchestrated alongside the surprise and memory capture.
